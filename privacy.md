# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 11, 2026

## What data does the bot access?

Cove Video Downloader reads message content in Discord channels where it is present. It scans messages for video URLs from supported platforms (YouTube, Reddit, Twitter/X, Instagram, TikTok, and others).

## What data does the bot store?

The bot does not store message content, user data, or personal information. When a video URL is detected, the bot extracts only the URL, downloads the video, re-uploads it to the Discord channel, and discards the temporary file. No message content or user information is retained after processing.

## What data is shared with third parties?

None. The bot does not share any data with third parties.

## How can I remove the bot's access to my data?

A server administrator can remove the bot from their server at any time by kicking or banning it. Once removed, the bot no longer has access to any messages in that server.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach the developer on Discord or open an issue on the project's GitHub repository.
